**The ultimate open-source collection of high-quality prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and other AI models.**

### ✨ Why prompts.free?
- Completely free forever
- No sign-up required
- Well-organized & regularly updated
- Works with all major AI models in 2026
- Save hours and get dramatically better AI results

2. Click any prompt
